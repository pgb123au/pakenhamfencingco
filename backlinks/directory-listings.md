## Long Business Description (250–500 words)

Pakenham Fencing is a fencing specialist serving Pakenham and the wider Cardinia Shire growth corridor, including Cardinia Lakes, Lakeside, Heritage Springs, Officer and Beaconsfield. We design, supply and install fencing built for the conditions this part of South-East Melbourne actually throws at it — exposed new-estate lots with high wind loads, reactive clay soils that move with the seasons, and bushfire-prone rural zones on the shire's fringes.

Our services cover the full range of residential, pool and rural fencing:

- Colorbond fencing — the corridor's most popular boundary choice for durability and low maintenance
- Timber paling fencing — including like-for-like replacement of the ageing original fences across the early-2000s estates
- Pool safety fencing in glass and aluminium — fully compliant with the Victorian pool and spa barrier regulations and AS 1926.1
- Rural and post-and-rail fencing for acreage and lifestyle blocks
- Driveway and pedestrian gates, plus automated gate openers

Cardinia's reactive clay is unforgiving on shallow footings, so we set posts to the correct depth and diameter for the soil and fence height — the detail that determines whether a fence is still straight in five years. On newer, exposed estate lots we account for wind loading in post spacing and panel selection. Every job is built to the relevant standards and laws: AS 1926.1 for pool safety barriers, AS 1725 for chain-link, the Cardinia Shire fencing local law, and the Fences Act 1968 (Vic) governing shared dividing fences. We also help homeowners navigate estate design covenants and the neighbour Fencing Notice process, so a boundary replacement doesn't turn into a dispute.

We work across the corridor's residential streets, the new release estates, and rural-residential properties on the edges of the shire. Whether it's a single failing boundary panel, a full estate-home re-fence, a compliant pool barrier ahead of a summer build, or a long rural run with an automated gate, we provide honest, current advice and a clear quote. Indicative 2026 pricing, supplied and installed, runs from around $90–$130 per lineal metre for Colorbond, $80–$120 for timber paling, $300–$600 for glass pool fencing, and $40–$80 for post-and-rail — with the final figure depending on height, access, ground conditions and removal of any old fence.

For a quote or to talk through the right fence for your block, contact Pakenham Fencing on (03) 9003 0108 or quotes@pakenhamfencingco.com.au, or visit https://pakenhamfencingco.com.au/. Serving Pakenham VIC 3810 and the Cardinia Shire growth corridor.
